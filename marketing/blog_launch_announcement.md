## The Problem With "Free" Prediction Platforms

Let's be real about how most prediction services work:

**They harvest your data.** Every pick you make, every model you build, every pattern you discover — collected and monetized. You're not the customer; you're the product.

**They sell to brokers.** Your betting insights get packaged and sold to sportsbooks, data brokers, and algorithmic trading firms. They use your edge to trade against you.

**They manipulate markets.** Some platforms even adjust their predictions based on where the money is flowing — using your collective wisdom against you to balance their books.

This is the dirty secret of "free" sports predictions. And it's why most serious bettors eventually feel like they're fighting two opponents: the sportsbook *and* the platform they trusted.
