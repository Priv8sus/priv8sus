## Engagement Rules

### Do
- Reply to mentions within 2 hours
- Quote tweet relevant NBA news with insight
- Engage authentically with betting community
- Retweet positive user feedback
- Acknowledge criticism openly

### Don't
- Post generic "check out our product" content
- Use engagement bait (#followforfollow, etc.)
- Spam trending hashtags
- Auto-DM new followers
- Delete negative comments (address them)

## Crisis Management

### If Negative Feedback
1. Acknowledge within 1 hour
2. Respond with specific solution
3. Take conversation to DM if needed
4. Post follow-up showing what was fixed

### If Platform Issues
1. Transparency first
2. Direct users to status page
3. Offer goodwill gesture if applicable
