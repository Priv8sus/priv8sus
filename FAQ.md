# Priv8sus FAQ

## Product Questions

**Q: What sports does Priv8sus support?**
A: We cover NBA, NFL, NCAA Basketball, NCAA Football, MLB, NHL, and major soccer leagues. Our player props predictions span all major US sports plus soccer.

**Q: What types of predictions do you offer?**
A: We offer multiple prediction types:
- **Player props** — points, rebounds, assists, home runs, touchdowns, and more
- **Game outcomes** — moneyline, spread, over/under
- **Live betting** — in-game predictions as action unfolds
- **Parlay recommendations** — ML-curated multi-leg picks

**Q: Can I compete with my friends?**
A: Yes! Create private leaderboards with friends, share predictions, and see who has the best track record. Our social features let you learn from the community while competing in private circles.

**Q: How accurate are your predictions?**
A: Our ML models are trained on historical NBA data. You can see accuracy metrics (MAE, calibration) on your dashboard. We're targeting >55% accuracy on points predictions at launch.

**Q: How do you track prediction accuracy?**
A: We track multiple metrics to measure prediction performance:
- **Hit Rate:** Percentage of predictions that were correct (e.g., over/under on player props)
- **MAE (Mean Absolute Error):** Average difference between predicted and actual values for continuous metrics (points, rebounds, assists)
- **ROI:** Return on investment based on paper trading results
- **Calibration Score:** How often predictions with X% confidence actually hit X% of the time

All metrics are displayed on your personal dashboard so you can track YOUR prediction performance over time.

**Q: Can I track my own betting record?**
A: Yes! Our paper trading feature lets you track hypothetical bets based on our predictions. You'll see:
- Total picks tracked
- Win/loss record
- ROI percentage
- Streak tracking (current and longest)
- Comparison to community averages

This helps you validate our predictions and build your own track record before risking real money at sportsbooks.

**Q: Is this a sportsbook? Can I place bets directly?**
A: No, Priv8sus is a prediction platform — not a sportsbook. We provide predictions and paper trading so you can test strategies. When you're ready, you can use those predictions at any legal sportsbook.

**Q: How is this different from ESPN PickCenter or SportsLine?**
A: We use ML models focused on player-level predictions (points/rebounds/assists) rather than game-level lines. We also offer parlay recommendations. Most importantly, we never harvest or sell your betting data.

## Privacy & Data Questions

**Q: What data do you collect?**
A: We collect what's needed to provide predictions: account info, the predictions you view, and your paper trading activity. We don't sell or share your betting patterns with brokers.

**Q: Will you sell my predictions to sportsbooks?**
A: No. Your predictions, models, and betting history stay private. We won't sell or share your data with anyone — including sportsbooks or data brokers.

**Q: What makes you "privacy-first"?**
A: Unlike many "free" platforms, we don't:
- Harvest your betting patterns to sell to brokers
- Use your predictions to build products that compete with you
- Manipulate our markets based on where the money is flowing

A: We use essential cookies for authentication. We don't run third-party tracking ads or data broker networks.

## Getting Started Questions

**Q: How do predictions work?**
A: Our ML models analyze historical NBA player stats and game data to predict tonight's player performances (points, rebounds, assists). You see predictions on your dashboard and can track how accurate they are over time.

**Detailed Process:**
1. **Data Collection:** We pull player stats from official NBA data sources, including season averages, recent performance trends, and matchup history
2. **Feature Engineering:** Our models process dozens of signals — player efficiency ratings, home/away performance, rest days, injury status, head-to-head matchups
3. **ML Prediction:** Multiple model types (regression, classification) generate predictions with confidence scores
4. **Confidence Scoring:** Each prediction includes a confidence level (Low/Medium/High) based on model agreement and data quality
5. **Delivery:** Daily predictions appear on your dashboard before game time

**Prediction Types Available:**
- **Player Props:** Points, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks, three-pointers
- **Game Outcomes:** Moneyline, spread, over/under
- **Parlay Recommendations:** Multi-leg picks curated by ML models

**Q: What is paper trading?**
A: Paper trading lets you test predictions without risking real money. You track hypothetical bets based on our ML models and see how you'd perform over time. Build your track record, earn streaks and badges, and validate strategies—all in a risk-free environment.

**Q: Paper trading vs real betting - what's the difference?**
A: Paper trading is purely simulated — you track "what if" bets based on predictions with virtual bankroll. Real betting involves actual money at legal sportsbooks. We provide the predictions; you decide when/where to act on them.

**Q: Why NBA only?**
A: NBA was the right starting point for MVP — strong data availability, passionate fanbase, and clear prediction signals. We'll expand based on user demand.

**Q: Is this legal?**
A: Priv8sus provides predictions and information — we're not a sportsbook and don't facilitate betting. Predictions are for entertainment and informational purposes. Please check your local regulations regarding sports betting.

**Q: Who built this?**
A: A small team of sports fans and privacy advocates who got tired of their data being sold back to them. We're backed by noVC, taking the long view on building a fair prediction market.
