## Best Practices

1. **Personalize:** Always reference something specific about the publication or a recent article
2. **Be Concise:** Busy journalists appreciate short emails
3. **Provide Value:** Offer something useful (interview, data, exclusive access)
4. **Timing:** Send Tuesday-Wednesday mornings for best open rates
5. **Track:** Use unique subject lines so you can identify opens
6. **Social:** Follow up on Twitter/LinkedIn after email outreach
