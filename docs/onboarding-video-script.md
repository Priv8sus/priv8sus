# User Onboarding Walkthrough Video Script

**Duration:** 90-120 seconds (1.5-2 minutes)
**Format:** Screen-record walkthrough with voiceover
**Use case:** Landing page embed, welcome email, onboarding email sequence

---

## Video Structure

| Segment | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Intro Hook | 5 sec | Capture attention, state value prop |
| Signup/Login | 15 sec | Lower barrier, show ease |
| Dashboard Tour | 30 sec | Core experience, how predictions work |
| Making First Pick | 30 sec | Action moment, engagement |
| Paper Trading | 20 sec | Risk-free practice pitch |
| Closing CTA | 10 sec | Call to action |

**Total: ~110 seconds**

---

## Scene-by-Scene Script

### Scene 1: Intro Hook (0:00-0:05)
*[Landing page hero section, Priv8sus logo, privacy badge visible]*

**VO:** "Stop guessing. Start winning. AI-powered NBA predictions that keep your data private."

*[Fade into signup screen]*

---

### Scene 2: Signup/Login (0:05-0:20)
*[Record: clicking "Sign Up" button, email field, password field, "Create Account" button]*

**VO:** "Create your free account in under 30 seconds. No credit card required."

*[Brief pause on filled form]*

**VO:** "Already have an account? Just sign in. You will be predicting in minutes."

---

### Scene 3: Dashboard Tour (0:20-0:50)
*[Dashboard visible. Guide viewer eye to key sections with subtle cursor highlighting]*

**VO:** "Welcome to your prediction dashboard."

*[Pan to games strip at top]*

**VO:** "Up top, you see today's NBA games. Every matchup with predictions is marked and ready."

*[Pan to prediction cards]*

**VO:** "Below that, your top predictions ranked by our confidence score. Each card shows the player's name, their predicted points, rebounds, and assists — compared against their season average."

*[Zoom into one prediction card, highlight confidence badge]*

**VO:** "The green badge means this is a Best Bet — our highest confidence prediction. These are the picks we stand behind."

*[Pan to accuracy sidebar]*

**VO:** "On the right, track our accuracy over time. We show you exactly how reliable our predictions are. No black box."

---

### Scene 4: Making First Prediction (0:50-1:20)
*[Click on a player card, player detail panel slides in]*

**VO:** "Want more detail? Click any player."

*[Point to prediction breakdown]*

**VO:** "You see the full prediction breakdown — points, rebounds, assists — with exactly how we calculated it."

*[Point to Best Bet highlight]*

**VO:** "And when we have high confidence, we label it as a Best Bet. This is where our edge lives."

*[Point to Paper Trade button]*

**VO:** "Paper trade button is right here. Practice with fake money before you risk anything real."

---

### Scene 5: Paper Trading Explanation (1:20-1:40)
*[Paper trading interface visible]*

**VO:** "Paper trading lets you test our predictions with zero risk. Track your win rate. Build confidence. When you are ready for real bets, you will know."

*[Show paper trade history/tracking]*

**VO:** "Every prediction we make, you can paper trade. See how our picks perform over time."

---

### Scene 6: Closing CTA (1:40-1:50)
*[Pull back to full dashboard, logo centered]*

**VO:** "Start free. Predict with confidence. Keep your secrets — at Priv8sus."

*[Fade to logo end card with tagline and URL]*

---

## Voiceover Notes

- **Tone:** Confident, straightforward — this is a tool for serious bettors
- **Pacing:** Match VO to on-screen action — never talk over UI transitions
- **Music:** Subtle low-key instrumental (sports/finance adjacent)
- **Privacy angle:** Emphasize "keep your data private" and "keep your secrets" as differentiator

---

## Screen Recording Tools

| Tool | Platform | Cost | Notes |
|------|----------|------|-------|
| **Loom** | Browser | Free tier | Easiest for quick recordings, includes link sharing |
| **OBS Studio** | Mac/PC | Free | Full control, higher production quality |
| **Camtasia** | Mac/PC | $150 | Professional editing built-in |
| **ScreenFlow** | Mac | $130 | Best Mac-native, easy editing |

**Recommendation:** Loom for quick iterations, ScreenFlow/OBS for polished final version

---

## Production Specifications

- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum (1920x1080 optimal)
- **Browser size:** 1280x720 or 1920x1080
- **Cursor:** Highlight key UI elements with subtle glow or cursor emphasis
- **Timestamps:** Optional in corner for viewer orientation
- **End card:** Logo + URL (priv8sus.com) + social handles
