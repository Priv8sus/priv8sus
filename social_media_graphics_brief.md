**Purpose:** Creative brief and copy for 5 launch announcement graphics

## Brand Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep Navy `#0D1B2A` — trust, privacy, security
- **Secondary:** Electric Blue `#1E88E5` — action, technology
- **Accent:** Bright Teal `#00BCD4` — energy, predictions
- **Alert/CTA:** Lime Green `#76FF03` — growth, wins
- **Background:** Dark charcoal `#1A1A2E` — premium feel
- **Text:** White `#FFFFFF` on dark, Navy `#0D1B2A` on light

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Montserrat Bold (or Inter Bold)
- **Body:** Open Sans Regular
- **Numbers/Stats:** Roboto Mono

### Logo Usage
- Lock icon + "Priv8sus" wordmark
- Always include `priv8sus.com` URL on final frame
- Lock icon = privacy differentiator
