# First User Interview Script

## Objective
Gather qualitative feedback from early adopters before launch to refine product positioning, identify pain points, and validate willingness to pay.

## Target
- 5 user interviews before launch
- Participants: early signups, beta testers, sports betting enthusiasts

---

## Screening Questions (2 min)

1. **"How often do you bet on sports?"**
   - Daily / Weekly / Monthly / Occasional
   - _Qualifies genuine users vs casual curiosity_

2. **"What sports do you bet on most?"**
   - NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, NCAA, etc.
   - _Ensures fit with product focus_

3. **"Do you currently use any sports betting tools or analytics?"**
   - Yes (which ones?) / No
   - _Identifies current solutions being used_

---

## Core Interview Questions (20 min)

### Motivation & Problem Awareness (Questions 4-5)

4. **"What's the hardest part about being a sports bettor?"**
   - Probe: information overload, bankroll management, finding value, tracking bets
   - _Identifies primary pain points the product addresses_

5. **"Have you ever felt like you had good information but couldn't act on it effectively?"**
   - Probe: What was the situation? What would have helped?
   - _Validates the core value proposition_

### Product Validation (Questions 6-7)

6. **"If you could have any tool to help you make better bets, what would it do?"**
   - _Open-ended feature discovery without leading_

7. **"I want to understand your dream sports betting assistant. What features does it have?"**
   - Probe: predictions, bankroll tracking, community insights, privacy
   - _Uncovers real priorities vs stated priorities_

### Willingness to Pay (Questions 8-9)

8. **"What would you pay monthly for a tool that genuinely improved your betting outcomes?"**
   - _anchoring if needed: "Would you pay $X? $Y?"_
   - _Establishes price sensitivity and value perception_

9. **"Have you paid for sports betting tools before? What did you get, and was it worth it?"**
   - Probe: What worked? What didn't?
   - _Validates paid tier positioning_

### Missing Features & Feedback (Question 10)

10. **"What's one thing you wish existed in sports betting tools today that nobody offers?"**
    - _Identifies whitespace opportunity_

---

## Closing (3 min)

11. **"Would you be willing to be a beta user and stay engaged as we build this?"**
    - Collect email/Discord if yes
    - _Builds early adopter pipeline_

12. **"Is there anyone else you know who would be interested in being an early tester?"**
    - _Referral pipeline for more interviews_

---

## Participant Recruitment Strategy

### Channel 1: Reddit Outreach
- Target subreddits: r/sportsbook, r/sportsbetting, r/nba
- Post: "Building a privacy-first sports betting tool - looking for early feedback"
- DM template for interested users (see Appendix A)

### Channel 2: Twitter/X
- Target: Users active in #sportsbetting, #NBA twitter
- Engage authentically before DM
- DM template (see Appendix A)

### Channel 3: Direct Email
- Contact early waitlist signups
- Personalized outreach referencing their signup context

### Target Demographics
- Active sports bettors (weekly+)
- NBA and NFL focused
- Privacy-conscious users
- Users frustrated with current tools

---

## Interview Format
- **Duration:** 25-30 minutes
- **Format:** Video call (Zoom/Google Meet) or voice call
- **Incentive:** Early access + lifetime discount for beta participants
- **Notes:** Record with consent, take notes during call
