# User Interview Script — Post-Launch Feedback

**Purpose:** Gather qualitative insights from early users to inform product development and monetization strategy.

**Interview Duration:** 15-20 minutes
**Target Users:** Early adopters who signed up during MVP launch

---

## Opening Questions (10)

1. What first brought you to our sports prediction product? What problem were you trying to solve?

2. Can you describe your experience getting started with the product? How easy was the onboarding?

3. What was the main feature or capability you were most excited to try?

4. When you first logged in, what was your first impression of the dashboard?

5. How does this product fit into your current sports betting routine? Is it a primary tool or supplementary?

6. What sports do you typically bet on, and how often?

7. What other sports prediction or analytics services have you used before?

8. What did you find most valuable about the product?

9. What fell short of your expectations?

10. If you could change one thing about the product today, what would it be?

---

## Sports Betting Habits (5)

11. How do you currently make your betting decisions — research, gut feeling, stats, or a mix?

12. What information sources do you rely on most when placing bets?

13. How much time do you spend researching a single bet?

14. Do you track your betting history? If so, how?

15. What's your typical bet size range, and what sports/leagues do you focus on?

---

## Prediction Accuracy Expectations (5)

16. What level of prediction accuracy would make you trust this product consistently?

17. How important is understanding *why* a prediction is made (explainability) versus just getting the prediction?

18. Would you find historical prediction performance data useful for your decisions?

19. How do you evaluate whether a prediction service is genuinely helpful versus lucky?

20. What types of predictions would be most valuable to you — game outcomes, player props, line movements?

---

## Pricing Willingness (3)

21. What would you expect to pay monthly for a premium sports prediction service?

22. Would you prefer a subscription model or pay-per-prediction? Why?

23. What features would make a $29/month subscription worthwhile to you?

---

## Closing Questions for Referrals

24. Can you think of anyone in your network who would benefit from this type of product?

25. What would make you recommend this product to a friend?

26. Would you be willing to participate in a follow-up interview as we develop new features? What topics would you want us to explore?
