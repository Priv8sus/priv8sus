## Priv8Sus Launches Privacy-First Sports Prediction Market, Redefining User Data Ownership in Sports Betting

*New NBA prediction platform lets users keep their edge — without algorithmic manipulation or data harvesting*

**April 2, 2026** — Priv8Sus today announced the launch of its privacy-first sports prediction market, debuting with NBA predictions and plans to expand to additional sports. Priv8Sus is built on a single principle: users' predictions belong to them, not to data brokers or algorithmic platforms.

The sports betting industry has a data problem. Most "free" prediction platforms harvest users' betting patterns, sell insights to brokers, and use those predictions to build products that benefit the platform — not the user. Priv8Sus was founded to change that dynamic.

> "Every time you use a prediction platform, you're likely giving away the very edge you've worked to build. We built Priv8Sus because we believe your predictions should stay private. Your models. Your insights. Your edge. Not harvested and resold."
> — Theo, CEO and Founder, Priv8Sus

### Key Features of Priv8Sus

- **Privacy-First Design**: User prediction models and betting data are never sold, shared, or used to power algorithmic trading against users
- **NBA Launch**: Starting with NBA moneyline, spreads, and parlay predictions
- **User Ownership**: Users retain full ownership of their prediction models and betting history
- **No Algorithmic Manipulation**: No hidden algorithms that adjust odds against user predictions
- **Transparent Markets**: Honest prediction markets where user insights have real value

### Market Opportunity

The sports betting prediction market has grown significantly, with millions of users seeking algorithmic edges. However, a growing backlash against data harvesting has created demand for privacy-conscious alternatives. Priv8Sus addresses this gap by providing a platform where users can predict, profit, and keep their secrets.
