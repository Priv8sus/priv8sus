# Priv8sus Onboarding Video Script

**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Format:** Screen-record walkthrough with voiceover
**Use case:** Landing page embed, welcome email, onboarding email sequence

---

## The 5 Key Moments

1. **Landing** — First impression, value proposition
2. **Sign Up** — Account creation (30 seconds)
3. **Welcome Screen** — Feature introduction, "Best Bet" hook
4. **Dashboard** — Core experience, how to read predictions
5. **First Pick** — Making a paper trade (action moment)

---

## 60-Second Script

### Opening (0:00–0:05)
*[Landing page visible, Priv8sus logo centered]*

**VO:** "Sports betting predictions that keep your data private. Welcome to Priv8sus."

---

### Sign Up / Login (0:05–0:15)
*[Screenrecord: clicking "Sign Up" button, filling in email/password, clicking "Create Account"]*

**VO:** "Create your free account in seconds. No credit card required."

*[Brief shot of the signup form]*

**VO:** "Already have an account? Just sign in. You're in within minutes."

---

### Welcome Screen (0:15–0:25)
*[After login, welcome modal appears showing: target icon, "Today's top predictions with confidence scores", chart icon, "Track our accuracy and improve your betting", money icon, "Paper trade to practice risk-free"]*

**VO:** "You'll see today's top NBA predictions, complete with our confidence score. Green badges mean our best bets — predictions we're most sure about."

*[User clicks "See My Best Bet"]*

---

### Dashboard Explanation (0:25–0:45)
*[Dashboard is now visible. Guide the viewer's eye to key sections]*

**VO:** "Here's your dashboard. Up top, you'll see today's games. Select any matchup to filter predictions by team."

*[Point to games strip]*

**VO:** "Below that, your top predictions ranked by confidence. Each card shows the player's name, their predicted points, rebounds, and assists — plus how that compares to their season average."

*[Point to a prediction card, then the confidence badge]*

**VO:** "On the right, you can see our track record — how accurate our predictions have been over time. We show you MAE and calibration scores so you know exactly how reliable our picks are."

*[Brief shot of the accuracy sidebar]*

---

### Making Your First Pick (0:45–0:55)
*[Click on a player card, player detail panel slides in]*

**VO:** "Want to see more detail? Click any player. You'll get their full prediction breakdown and our best bet recommendation."

*[Point to the "Best Bet" highlight]*

**VO:** "Paper trading button is right here — practice with fake money before you risk anything real."

---

### Help & Wrap (0:55–1:00)
*[Camera pulls back to show full dashboard]*

**VO:** "Need help at any point? Click the question mark button for a guided tour. Start predicting, profit, and keep your secrets — at Priv8sus."

*[Fade to logo, tagline appears: "Predict. Profit. Keep your secrets."]*

---

## Voiceover Notes

- Keep tone confident and straightforward — this is a tool for people who bet seriously
- Emphasize the privacy angle ("keep your data private", "keep your secrets") as differentiator
- Pace the VO to match on-screen action — never talk over UI changes
- Music: subtle, low-key instrumental (sports/finance adjacent)

## Visual Production Notes

- Record at 1080p minimum
- Use browser at 1280x720 or 1920x1080
- Highlight key UI elements with subtle glow or cursor emphasis
- Timestamps in corner of screen optional (adds structure for viewers)
- End card: logo + URL (priv8sus.com) + social handles
