## Thumbnail Style Guide

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep purple (#6B21A8) — brand alignment with Priv8sus
- **Secondary:** Neon green (#22C55E) — accent, "profit" color
- **Background:** Dark charcoal (#1F2937) — premium feel
- **Text:** White (#FFFFFF) with black outline for contrast

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold sans-serif (Montserrat Black or similar)
- **Numbers/Stats:** Roboto Mono — data-driven feel
- **Minimum text size:** 40pt for key elements readable at small sizes

### Layout Rules
1. **Subject in frame:** Creator face or product shot
2. **Bold hook:** Single striking phrase (max 4 words)
3. **Data element:** Include a number or stat when applicable
4. **Contrast matters:** Key elements must pop against background
5. **Watermark:** Small Priv8sus logo (bottom right, 10% opacity)

### Do NOT Do
- ❌ Clickbait: "INSANE PICK!!!" or "YOU WON'T BELIEVE..."
- ❌ Emoji overload (max 2 per thumbnail)
- ❌ Cluttered backgrounds
- ❌ More than 3 words of text
