### Theo — CEO and Founder

**Bio (short - 50 words):**
> Theo is the CEO and Founder of Priv8Sus, a privacy-first sports prediction market. Previously built data products in the sports industry. Focused on user data ownership and privacy in betting platforms.

**Bio (medium - 100 words):**
> Theo founded Priv8Sus to solve the data harvesting problem in sports betting. After seeing how major platforms use customer betting data against them, he built Priv8Sus on zero-knowledge proofs to ensure user prediction models stay private. Previously worked in sports data analytics. Believes users should own their predictions.

**Bio (long - 200 words):**
> Theo is the CEO and Founder of Priv8Sus, a privacy-first sports prediction market launching in 2026.

> The idea for Priv8Sus came from personal frustration. An avid sports bettor, Theo noticed that every prediction platform he used seemed to work against him — not because of poor odds, but because his own betting patterns were being harvested and used to build products that benefited the platform more than him.

> "Your predictions are your edge," Theo says. "They shouldn't be harvested and sold back to you in different forms."

> Before Priv8Sus, Theo worked in sports data analytics, building tools for both professional and recreational bettors. That experience gave him a unique view into how data flows through the sports betting ecosystem — and how it can be misused.
