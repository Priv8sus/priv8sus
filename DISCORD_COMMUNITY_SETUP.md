# Discord Community Setup Guide

**Product:** priv8sus - Sports Betting Prediction Platform  
**Owner:** CPO  
**Date:** 2026-04-02  
**Purpose:** User engagement and community building post-launch

---

## Discord Server Structure

### Category: WELCOME
- **#welcome** - Server announcements and launch news
- **#rules** - Community guidelines and terms

### Category: GENERAL
- **#general-chat** - Off-topic discussion
- **#introductions** - New member introductions

### Category: PREDICTIONS
- **#nba-picks** - Daily NBA predictions and discussion
- **#paper-trading** - Simulated betting strategies
- **#prediction-results** - Tracking prediction accuracy

### Category: SUPPORT
- **#help** - Technical support and troubleshooting
- **#faq** - Frequently asked questions

### Category: FEEDBACK
- **#feature-requests** - Product improvement ideas
- **#bug-reports** - Report issues
- **#success-stories** - User wins and testimonials

---

## Welcome Bot & Rules

### Bot Features
1. Auto-role assignment (Member, Premium tester)
2. Welcome message with link to app
3. Rule acceptance verification
4. Spam/flood protection

### Community Rules
1. No real-money betting discussion
2. Be respectful - no personal attacks
3. No spam or self-promotion
4. Keep predictions discussion focused
5. No NSFW content
6. Help each other - pay it forward

## 5 Post-Launch Discussion Prompts

1. **"Which NBA player's stats surprised you most this week?"** - Engage users about prediction accuracy

2. **"Paper trading strategy check-in: What's your current win rate?"** - Share paper trading results

3. **"Privacy in sports betting - why does it matter to you?"** - Reinforce our unique positioning

4. **"Weekend NBA slate preview - drop your bold predictions here"** - Build engagement around games

5. **"Feature reveal: What's your #1 requested feature?"** - Drive feedback and feature requests
